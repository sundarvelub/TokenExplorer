Rules of thumb

~4 chars = 1 token (English)
1 page ~500 words ≈ 750 tokens
1 min speech ≈ 175 tokens
1 image ≈ 1,000–2,000 tokens
Code: ~3 chars/token
Non-Latin scripts: ~2 chars/token
Numbers/symbols: 1–2 chars/token
Input tokens cost less than output
